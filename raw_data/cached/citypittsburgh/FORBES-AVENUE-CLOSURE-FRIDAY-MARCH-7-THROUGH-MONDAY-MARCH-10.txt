FORBES AVENUE CLOSURE:  FRIDAY, MARCH 7 THROUGH MONDAY, MARCH 10
Pittsburgh, PA – A section of Forbes Avenue in Pittsburgh’s Oakland neighborhood will be fully closed to traffic from 7 PM today, March 7, 2025, until 5 AM on Monday, March 10, 2025. Reopening in time for the morning commute.
The closure will take place between South Craig Street and Morewood Avenue to allow Carnegie Mellon University to complete crane-based work in the area.
Motorists and cyclists will be detoured via Morewood Street, Fifth Avenue, and South Craig Street to access Forbes Avenue in both the inbound and outbound directions.
We encourage drivers and cyclists to plan accordingly and use alternate routes during this time.
