BigBurgh.comis a website that connects users with free, low-barrier resources in the City of Pittsburgh. Its aim is to help people having a difficult time, especially those experiencing homelessness, access the things they need.
BigBurgh.com: https://www.bigburgh.com
Need to find food, shelter, medical care, employment assistance, or more? BigBurgh is here for you 24/7, 365 days a year. Click on the Live Help button to connect with a social service professional if you or someone you know is in immediate need.
