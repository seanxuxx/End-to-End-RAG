Grab a blanket or lawn chair and head out for an unforgettable evening of free cinema under the stars! Dollar Bank Cinema in the Park entertains throughout the summer in various park settings. Movies begin at dusk.
In June and July, return to your favorite movie location at Highland Park, Riverview Park, Schenley Park Flagstaff Hill, Schenley Plaza, Arsenal Park in Lawrenceville, Banksville Park, Brookline Memorial Park, Grandview Park in Mt. Washington, McBride Park in Lincoln Place, Ormsby Park in South Side, and West End/Elliott Overlook.
In August, the cinema series concludes with a monthlong schedule of Pop-Up Movies at a variety of scenic parks located throughout the City of Pittsburgh. To learn when/where movies take place, visit pittsburghpa.gov/events, like@PghEventsOffice on Facebook,@PghEventsOffice on Instagram, and follow@PghEventsOffice on X.
The Sunday, July 28, movie at Schenley Plaza - "The Peanut Butter Falcon" (PG-13) - will be presented in partnership with Film Pittsburgh as a preview of the upcoming ReelAbilities Film Festival.
Three engagements will offer headphones for descriptive audio services: "Wonka" (PG) June 23 at Schenley Plaza, "Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny" (PG-13) July 20 at Riverview Park, and "The Peanut Butter Falcon" (PG-13) July 28 at Schenley Plaza.
For more information, follow CitiParks Office of Special Events on@PghEventsOffice on X,@PghEventsOffice on Facebookor@PghEventsOffice on Instagram.
Please Note: Inclement weather may cause cancellations.For cancellation updates follow@PghEventsOffice on Facebookand@PghEventsOffice on X.
Dollar Bank Cinema in the Park is presented by the Citiparks Office of Special Events.
Please Note: The City of Pittsburgh does not allow campaigning or signature gathering at any of its event locations.
