Pool Tags are available atopenpool locations.
If you want to swim in a CitiParks pool you will need to pay a daily admission feeorpurchase a pool tag.
This is a great option for people who want to spend a lot of time in the water.
Pool tags must be purchased at one of our 15 open pool locations. Children 15 and under, who are City residents, are able to swim for free.
There are no refunds for pool tags. Replacements for lost or stolen tags must be purchased.
If you just want to swim for a day, you can pay for a daily admission ticket at any pool.
Save Time- Fill out your 2024 Pool Tag Application before you head to the pool to purchase your pool tags!Please note: these applications are only accepted in person at one of our outdoor pools from June - September.
