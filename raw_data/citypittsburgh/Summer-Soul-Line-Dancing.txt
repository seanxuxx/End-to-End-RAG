In This Section
Whether you are an experienced dancer or just want to learn some new moves, step out to an area park for the ever-popular Summer Soul Line Dancing. Sessions incorporate R&B music in free classes that begin with basic steps and finish with more advanced moves.
For more information, follow CitiParks Office of Special Events on @PghEventsOffice on X , @PghEventsOffice on Facebook or @PghEventsOffice on Instagram .
Weather Updates: Inclement weather may cause cancellations. For cancellation updates follow CitiParks Office of Special Events on @PghEventsOffice on Facebook and @PghEventsOffice on X .
