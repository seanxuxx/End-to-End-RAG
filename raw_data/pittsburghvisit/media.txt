VisitPittsburgh's Online News Room is a centralizedinformation resourcefor all news media. OurCommunications staffgladly assists qualified travel, trade and news journalists with a number of helpful services including scheduling interviews; organizing small-grouppress tours; arranging itineraries and facility visits to arts + cultural institutions, hotels and restaurants, and select tours and events. The team also provides fact-checking services and background information on Pittsburgh, as well as ideas for travel-related stories or information related to convention + meeting services and trends.
